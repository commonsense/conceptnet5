===Etymology===
From {{etyl|enm|en}} {{term|example|lang=enm}}, from {{etyl|fro|en}} {{term|essample|lang=fro}} (French: {{term|exemple|lang=fr}}), from {{etyl|la|en}} {{term|exemplum||literally what is taken out (as a sample), a sample, pattern, specimen, copy for imitation, etc.|lang=la}}, from {{term/t|la|eximō||take out}}, from {{term|ex||out}} + {{term|emo|emō|buy; acquire}}; see [[exempt]]. Compare [[ensample]], [[sample]], [[exemplar]]. Displaced native [[Middle English]] {{term|bisne||lang=enm}}, {{term|forbus|forbus, forbusen|example, model, template, exemplar|lang=enm}} (from [[Old English]] {{term/t|ang|bīsen}}, {{term|forebisen|forebīsen, forebȳsen|example, model, template, exemplar|lang=ang}}) and [[Middle English]] {{term|byspel||example, proverb|lang=enm}} (from [[Old English]] {{term/t|ang|bīspel}}).
