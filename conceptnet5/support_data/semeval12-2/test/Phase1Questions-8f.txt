Question 8(f) CAUSE-PURPOSE: Instrument:Goal

Consider the following word pairs:

anesthetic:numbness
ballast:stability
camouflage:concealment

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

an X cannot have attribute Y; Y is antithetical to being X
a Y receives an item/knowledge/service from X
X is intended to produce Y
X may be divided into Y

Correct Answer:

X is intended to produce Y
